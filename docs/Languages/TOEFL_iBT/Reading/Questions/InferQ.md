## 题目识别

> Paragraph 2 **implies** that which of the following...
> 
> Which of the following claims about ... can be **inferred** from paragraph 5?
> 
> What can be **inferred** from paragraph 1 about...

- infer/imply/support/suggest/conclude

## 方法技巧

- 从题干找定位词
- 去原文寻找相应语句
- 再查看对应选项
- 不是**先看选项！！！**

## 常规推理 & 对比推理

- 类型、特质对比
	- 不同于 A，B 是 ……
	- 不同于 A，B 因其 ……
- 时间自身对比
	- after/since/now
	- before/prior to
